About Me

Jane Vo is a coding bootcamp student originally from Houston, Texas, but now living in Austin, Texas. She is an Assistant Director of Admissions at The University of Texas at Austin. She facilitates the processing and technical aspects of the undergraduate application and appeal review process. She is proficient in utilizing systems such as Slate, Mainframe, and JIRA. In her spare time, she attends coding bootcamp courses and has written course projects using HTML, CSS and Javascript.
